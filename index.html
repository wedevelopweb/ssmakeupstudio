About Shweta

Professional Makeup Artist with a Passion for Beauty

Your Beauty, My Expertise

Shweta Singh is a professional makeup artist based in Aligarh with 4+ years of experience in bridal and editorial makeup. Certified from PARUL GARG, Shweta blends classic techniques with modern trends to create elegant looks that last all day. She has worked with brides across UP and Delhi NCR and specializes in natural, camera-ready finishes.

With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of diverse skin tones and face structures, Shweta ensures every client feels confident and beautiful. Her work is characterized by flawless blending, long-lasting formulas, and a personalized approach that brings out the best in every individual.
